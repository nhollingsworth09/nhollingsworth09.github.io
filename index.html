About

The first thing that may have caught your eye is the background image. Though I was born in Brooklyn, NY, I grew up on the beautiful island of Carriacou, Grenada where I completed most of my primary and secondary education. The picture overlooks my hometown of Hillsborough, the largest town on the island. It is here that my love of math and science started and grew during my teenage years.

If I'm not watching a movie or trying to convince you to watch my most recent Netflix addiction, then I'm looking for the next adventure to find, skill to learn, or project to undertake. From learning to play a new instrument to learning a new programming language, I approach all challenges with a clear plan and a positive attitude.

What I offer

I specialize in business analytics and data mining. During both my undergraduate and professional experience, I have been allowed the opportunity to work on a few technical projects. Leveraging my knowledge and extending into personal projects, my aim is to solve problems where the solution is not obvious and success not guaranteed. With this in mind, I've developed a work style that emphasizes what can be learned from the process of finding a solution in addition to producing a final result.
