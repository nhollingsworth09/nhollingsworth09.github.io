About

Though born in Brooklyn, NY, Nykosi was raised on the picturesque island of Carriacou, Grenada, where he completed his primary and secondary education. As if growing up around white sand beaches and turquoise waters wasn’t enough, he desired more and decided to indulge in his love for Mathematics and Science. He returned to America where he matriculated at St. Peter’s University in New Jersey, receiving his B.S. in Mathematics, with a minor in Physics.

Nykosi brings ingenuity to data and actuarial science by seeking innovative strategies that incorporates solution driven techniques. His passion and creativity have allowed him to participate in technical projects, which encouraged him to develop a personal work flow that optimizes learning and success.

When he’s not studying for the next actuarial exam, Nykosi can be found pursuing his interests in snowboarding, skateboarding, skydiving, jet-skiing, soccer, cricket, carpentry, and playing guitar and piano. Also, he can ever so often be found binging on his new Netflix craze.
